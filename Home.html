Photo Story

In the Morning, before I had to meet a professor, to finalize for my thesis. I was not sure will it be the right decision to take or not? I was brushing my teeth, listening to "No Direction", looking down to empty roads, stuck in my thoughts. I had to meet him in evening, we talked and discussed and then I felt, its not such a complicated thing. He was excited to have me in for project which I felt is the nicest thing. It started raining afterwards. I took a walk in campus, with a feeling of burden being lifted, like my worries were washed away with the rain. The question if it is right thing for me?, Is this what i really want? What will I do later on after this? all of this is still in me but who knows what will happen. all i want is to enjoy and do it with my utmost sincerity.
